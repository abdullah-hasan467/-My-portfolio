IoT-based Smart Poultry Farming

The IoT-based Smart Poultry Farming Project utilizes sensors and connected devices to monitor and manage various aspects of poultry farming remotely. By integrating IoT technology, farmers can track environmental conditions such as temperature, humidity, and air quality in real-time. Automated systems can regulate these parameters, optimize feeding schedules, and provide alerts for potential issues, enhancing efficiency and productivity in poultry farming operations.
